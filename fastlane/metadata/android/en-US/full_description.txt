Hibiki is an unofficial Android client for anime browsing and playback.

Browse a catalog of featured titles, trending titles, and recent updates. Search by title, use filters, and open detailed pages with descriptions, ratings, genres, screenshots, and related titles.

Choose a watch source and episode, then watch anime in the built-in player. Adjust playback quality and speed, enable autoplay for the next episode, and use opening and ending skip controls.

Keep track of your watch progress, continue from the last opened title, and organize a local library using watching, planned, completed, dropped, on-hold, favorite, and saved categories. Episodes can be saved for local playback.

The interface is available in English and Russian.
